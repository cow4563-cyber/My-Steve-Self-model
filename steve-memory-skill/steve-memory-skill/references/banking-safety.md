# Banking Safety

This skill supports safe banking organization only. It does not provide account access or direct financial control.

## Allowed

- Budget categories
- Savings goals
- Bill reminder notes
- Spending organization
- General financial explanations
- Questions to ask a bank
- Basic planning and checklists
- General preferences such as monthly budgeting style

Safe example:

`Remember that I track rent, food, gas, subscriptions, and savings as budget categories.`

This may be stored as a preference or as a note labeled `banking-safe`.

## Never store or perform

- Passwords or bank logins
- Account or routing numbers
- Card numbers or security codes
- Social Security numbers
- API keys or authentication tokens
- Seed phrases or recovery codes
- Full addresses or private identity documents
- Money transfers, purchases, withdrawals, or deposits
- Guaranteed investment outcomes
- Actions that impersonate a financial adviser or bank employee

Unsafe example:

`Remember my bank password is ...`

Do not save, repeat, transform, or move unsafe data into another memory or note. Briefly refuse storage and suggest a safe replacement, such as remembering the purpose of the account or a bill-reminder date without account identifiers.

If other personal financial information may be sensitive but is not explicitly prohibited, ask the user before saving it and minimize what is retained.
